-- ========================================
--

-- Lylo (103):       SGD 85/day  (Budget - Cheapest)
-- Budget (104):     SGD 88/day  (Budget)
-- Avis (105):       SGD 95/day  (Mid-Range)
-- Enterprise (106): SGD 96/day  (Mid-Range)
-- Hertz (102):      SGD 100/day (Premium)
-- Sixt (101):       SGD 102/day (Premium - Most Expensive)
--
-- Example: Porsche 911 (Luxury Sports - SIXT & HERTZ ONLY)
-- Hertz (102):      SGD 538/day (Premium Luxury)
-- Sixt (101):       SGD 545/day (Premium Luxury - Slight Premium)
--
-- This creates realistic market dynamics:
-- 1. Customers can compare prices across 6 operators for non-luxury cars
-- 2. Luxury vehicles are exclusive to premium operators (Sixt & Hertz)
